Three days in. I want you to know how Panda Desiiignare actually happened.

The first three tracks I made — gone. Scrapped. They were trying too hard to sound like someone else. The fourth one, Chef Lu Stew, was the first time I trusted the prompt and let Suno cook. "Bay-area holy stew, brand new child of God, gospel hip-hop, dusty samples." Hit play and didn't touch a knob.

Everything after that came from the same place: write the kingdom, let the machine flex, no drug references, no body talk, no malice. Five more tracks fell out in 48 hours.
